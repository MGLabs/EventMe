EventMe is an Android application listing all the events within a certain area.
It gives special attention to what we might call “minor” events. 
Most people have a talent, a passion, a dream or anything where they excel without that necessarily to 
be their profession or their primary source of income. 
They perform often remaining in the anonymity. The young band playing in a pub, or the aspiring writer 
presenting her writings in the local bookshop. 
Sport competitions, music events, local exhibitions and fairs; EventMe will give them visibility 
by providing a one of a kind platform where anyone will be able to post their own events. 

EventMe will be particularly useful in small areas and/or for whoever can't make it to
the big promotional mainstreams.

EventMe is working on progress.
